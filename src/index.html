How batch import works
1. Each photo is passed through Apple Vision OCR
2. Handwriting errors are corrected automatically
3. Text is split into 500-char chunks and embedded
4. Everything is stored in your local ChromaDB
5. Large collections (100+ pages) may take 10–20 minutes
